Some introductory words


On this part of our platform you find the OpenEnergy database (oedb) and the fact sheets. Both are already usable though still under continuous improvement. They were developed following the requirement to improve the transparency of energy system models and their outputs as well as the reproducibility of their scenario results. The fact sheets aim to give an overview of the existing models to modelers and other stakeholders interested in energy system modelling. Moreover, together with the database they are meant to provide the base to discuss various scenarios.
